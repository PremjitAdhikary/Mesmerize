Sin Cos Both

From Wikipedia: In mathematics, a unit circle is a circle with unit radius.

Trigonometric functions sine and cosine of an angle θ can be defined on a Unit Circle.

Let (x,y) be a point on the Unit Circle.

Let θ be the angle the ray from (0,0) to (x,y) makes with +ve x-axis.

Then we have:

  • cos θ = x
  • sin θ = y
