Mathematical Rose

It's a curve shaped like a rose.

The general equation is:

r = cos(kθ)
x = r cos(θ)
y = r sin(θ)
where k = n/d and θ changes from 0 to 2π.

The forms become more interesting when an offset c is added such that:

r = cos(kθ + c)

Triofolium: n = 3, d = 1
Quadrifolium: n = 2, d = 1

Maurer Rose

A Maurer rose consists of the 361 points defined by the following:

(x,y) = (sin(nk), k), where k = 0,d,2d,3d,...,360d and d is in degrees

The above points are connected by 360 lines.

Play with the sliders and try to form the rose in the preview.
