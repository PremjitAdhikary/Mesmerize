Consider this as an extension/spin-off of another of my project KombatSim.

In that project a number of different fighters are made to fight with each other. The idea being to touch upon all design patterns. The simulated fights were logged out into json files and the same is read here to animate them.

Consider this as My Coding Universe (MCU) where I aim to have cross overs among my projects.

Now to explain the controls...

  • Fight: Select type of fight.
    • Fair: Where both fighters have equal opportunity to win.
    • Biased: Where one of the fighter has huge advantage.
  • Fighter Class: Select class of fighter.
    • Amateur: The most basic of fighters.
    • Pro: Much more stronger and efficient than the amateurs.
    • Enhanced: Pro fighters enhanced with armor/weapon etc.
    • Super: These are fighter with godly powers.
  • Fighter: Select a fighter from the class.
  • Vs: Select a vs fighter from the class.
  • Select Fight: Select among available fights.
  • Load the selected fight.
  • Play!!! You can also use the same button to pause/unpause a fight.

Let's look at the interface itself. Pretty self explanatory isn't it?

Little bit about the fighters fighting here.

  • Fighters in Amateur Class:
    • Nerd: Weak but fast. Can Kick and Evade.
    • Bully: Strong but slow. Can Punch and Block.
    • Captain: Balanced. Can Punch, Kick and Block.
  • Fighters in Pro Class (they have a secondary attribute Stamina):
    • Boxer: Strongest but slowest among pros. Can Punch and Block. Has a special attack Cross.
    • Karateka: Most balanced. Can Punch, Kick and Block. Has a special attack Chop.
    • Taekwondo: Fastest but weakest among pros. Can Kick and Evade. Has a special attack Flying Kick.
  • Enhanced Fighters (these are basically enhanced Pro Class Fighters):
    • E Boxer: A much stronger Boxer.
    • Ninja: An enhanced Taekwondo with additional agility, weapon and projectiles. Ninja's weapon, Nun-Chuck, doesnt restrict him from using his original attack abilities, but on the contrary adds to it. Along with the Taekwondo attack and defend abilities, Ninja is able to attack with Nunchuck Swing and throw Shurikens.
    • Samurai: An enhanced Karateka with armor and weapon. The sword restricts him from using his original attack abilities, but gives him new abilities. The new attacks are Sword Cut and Sword Slash.
  • Super Class consists of Heroes and Mages. Heroes use a secondary attribute Mojo to execute powers. Mages have Mana to cast spells.
    • Superman: Strongest among all Heroes and quite fast too. He can Punch and Block. His powers are Heat Vision (does considerable damage and burns the opponent) and Freeze Breath (slows down the opponent).
    • Flash: Fastest among all Heroes and quite strong too. He can Kick and Evade. His powers are Arc Lightning (shocks the enemy and renders him useless) and Haste (a self cast which increases the Flash's speed by three times).
    • Batman: Only Hero without any powers. Weakest and slowest among all Heroes. But makes up for it with his Batsuit and array of gadgets. He can Punch and Block. His armor can absorb a sizeable portion of the damage to him. His gadgets incude Batarang, Thermite Pellets (burns the enemy), Freeze Grenades (slows down the enemy), Stun Pellets (shocks the enemy), Gas Pellets (poisons the enemy), Mana Burners (works wonders against the mages), Kryptonite (you know who this is for), Heal Pill (to regenerate Batman's Life).
    • Elemental: A Mage well versed with Elemental spells. He has 2 offensive and 2 defensive spells in his arsenal. Flame Breath to burn and Ice Blast to slow his enemy. His defensive spells cast an aura which stays active around the mage. This aura causes any attacker to either burn (in case the aura is Fire Shield) or to chill (in case it's Frozen Wall) every time the attacker attacks.
    • Dark: A Mage adept at Dark Spells. Just like Elemental, Dark also has a defensive spell to cast Thorns Curse aura. Everytime opponent attacks, this curse weakens him. Point being, longer the fight, the weaker the opponent gets. His offensive spells incude Paralyze which renders his opponent paralyzed for a certain duration, Bleed with which opponent keeps losing his life over a period of time and Life Steal with which the Dark mage replenishes his own life by stealing his opponents.
