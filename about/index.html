About The Site

One of my first projects had a lot of animation and event handling in it. I was always fascinated by it. Always wanted to explore more.

Then I came across these wonderful videos by Daniel Shiffman. That was the spark I needed!

Obviously most of the segments here are direct reproduction or highly inspired from the above mentioned collection. But this is my learning phase, and hope is that I will have have something up pretty interesting on my own.

The way the segments are arranged is a bit haphazard. I will keep adding as and when I fancy something to animate. While some of the segments will be quick, some will be a project on it's own and I will keep on modifying them in future.
