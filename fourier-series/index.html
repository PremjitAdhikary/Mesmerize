Square Wave Sawtooth Pulse Triangle

A Fourier series is a periodic function composed of harmonically related sinusoids, combined by a weighted summation. That's Wikipedia by the way.

Essentially, it is a sum of multiple sin (or cosine) waves of various amplitudes and/or frequencies.

Few series depicted here.

Square Wave Series:
 Csin(x) + Csin(3x)/3 +Csin(5x)/5 + ...

Sawtooth Series:
 Csin(x) + Csin(2x)/2 +Csin(3x)/3 + ...

Pulse Series:
 Csin(x) + Csin(2x) +Csin(3x) + ...

Triangle Series:
 Csin(x) - Csin(3x)/9 +Csin(5x)/25 + ...
